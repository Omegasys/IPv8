FAQ
Is IPv8 a replacement for IPv6?

No. It is an overlay and experimental protocol.

Why Base64 addressing?

Used for representation, not actual packet transport.

Is SHA-256 encryption?

No. It is a hashing function.

Does IPv8 replace TLS?

No. TLS operates at a higher layer.

Can IPv8 control routing paths?

Only within controlled or overlay environments.

Is Tor built into IPv8?

No. It is integrated as an optional overlay.

Why dynamic IP per connection?

To improve privacy and reduce tracking.
